World Settings — reorganized
Today the tab is one long undifferentiated scroll. I keep the four essentials — world extent, world height, sea level, show water (plus seed, the world's identity) — pinned in a World block at the top, and group the rest into Water, Build & Preview, and Outputs. Three ways to present those groups: 1a segmented sub-tabs, 1b collapsible sections, 1c collapsible with the heavy Water group clustered under its own toggles. I recommend 1c.
1a Segmented sub-tabs
World pinned on top; the rest split into a Water / Build / Output switcher. Most compact — one group in focus, no scrolling.
